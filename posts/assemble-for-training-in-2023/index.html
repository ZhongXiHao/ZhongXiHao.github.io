Assemble For Training In 2023

This is an album of assemble for training in 2023. Start on 27/1/2023, and end on 31/1/2023. The training took place in Taipa Stadium and Lou Hau High School Macao. Except the third day (29/1/2023), took place in Lin Fong Sports Ground and Flora Garden.
